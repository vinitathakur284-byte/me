My Story

"I didn't set out to build a women's health brand. I set out to build something real."

I started my career in 2019 in domestic and international marketing — working across pharma, FMCG, and consumer brands, helping them enter new markets across Europe, Africa, and Asia. I've represented brands at global trade platforms including CPhI, Medexpo, Iphex, and Ipheb, with deep experience in market entry strategy, cross-border partnerships, and brand internationalisation.

Somewhere along the way, I started noticing a pattern: the most underserved consumers in every market I worked in were women. And the most underinvested category in Indian startups — despite the massive market — was women's health.

That gap became my obsession.

Aurang India — my company — was born from that obsession. And CycleAlign, our first brand under it, came from watching brilliant women in leadership burn out because no one had ever connected their hormonal health to their performance.

I'm building what I wish had existed. And I'm doing it from Delhi — because the next great women's health brands won't just come from Silicon Valley or London. They'll come from here.
